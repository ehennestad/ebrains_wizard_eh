Thank you for choosing EBRAINS to share your research data. In this form, you can describe key aspects of your dataset so that other researchers will be able to find, reuse and cite your work. You can use the navigation bar above to explore the different sections and categories of metadata collected through this form.

While filling out the form, please remember to consider all data related to the dataset that you wish to publish on EBRAINS. Once you complete the form, metadata describing your dataset will be curated according to the openMINDS standard.

Note: If you are interrupted or unable to complete the form in one session, you can download the form as a JSON using the "Download form data" button on the top of the page, and then re-upload it later to continue where you left off.
